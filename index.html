Pricing

What you will get

  • Comprehensive Member Profiles – Track personal details, class attendance, payment history, competition results, and all communication in one place.
  • Flexible Payment Options – Offer reoccurring memberships, one-time class passes, and periodic payment plans tailored to your club’s needs.
  • Dynamic Scheduling – Easily update class schedules in real-time, display them on your Fitofan club page, and share instantly across social media.
  • Seamless Attendance Tracking – Check-in athletes via QR code scan, name search, or selection from the attendance list.
  • Hassle-Free Payments – Members can pay for classes online through your Fitofan club page or in person at the studio.
  • Inventory & Online Store Management – Sell equipment, apparel, and other products both in-studio and through your online shop.
  • Mobile App for Instant Communication – The Fitofan app (available on iOS & Android) keeps club members connected with quick messaging and updates.
  • User-Friendly & Intuitive Interface – Manage your entire sports organization with ease using our sport-focused social media and all-in-one management tools.
